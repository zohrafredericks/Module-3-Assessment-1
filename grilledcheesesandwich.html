Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Classic golden and cheesy sandwich

Prep time: 10 mins

Yield: 2 sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice, butter-side down, on the skillet. Add cheese slices, then top with the other bread slice, butter-side up.
  4. Cook until golden brown, then flip and cook the other side until cheese is melted.
